▶ QuickPrompt

Free online teleprompter — no sign-up, your script never leaves this device

Tip: Ctrl+Enter to start. While prompting: tap = pause/resume · Space play/pause · ↑↓ speed · +− font size · M mirror · G guide · F fullscreen · R restart · Esc exit

How it works

  1. Paste your script into the box above.
  2. Press Start prompting. After a short countdown, the text scrolls automatically.
  3. Tap the screen to pause or resume. Adjust speed and font size from the bottom bar while you read.

Features

  • Private by design — everything runs in your browser. The script is saved only on this device.
  • Mirror mode for beam-splitter teleprompter rigs.
  • Smooth scrolling with live speed and font-size control, a reading guide and a progress bar.
  • Works everywhere — phone, tablet (great on iPad) and desktop. Keeps the screen awake while prompting.
  • Free, no account — no upload, no watermark, no time limit.

FAQ

Is my script uploaded anywhere?

No. QuickPrompt makes no network requests with your text. The script lives in your browser's local storage on this device only. Note that clearing your browser data will delete it, so keep your original copy elsewhere.

What is mirror mode?

It flips the text horizontally (⇋ button, or press M) so it reads correctly through the beam-splitter glass of a physical teleprompter rig.

Does it work on iPad and iPhone?

Yes — it runs in Safari and Chrome on iPad, iPhone and Android. On supported devices the screen stays awake automatically while prompting.

Can I use a keyboard or presenter remote?

Yes. Space plays/pauses and the arrow keys adjust speed and position. Most Bluetooth presenter remotes send Page Up / Page Down, which jump the script backward and forward.
